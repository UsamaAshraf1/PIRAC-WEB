About PRAC

Our Vision

To establish Pakistan as a sovereign, globally credible force in AI, robotics, and automation — through disciplined execution, validated systems, and an empowered national workforce.

Our Mission

Define high-impact national problems. Build minimum viable systems. Deploy with real users. Validate with real data. Scale only what works. Build 100,000 industry-ready professionals by 2030.

Execution Model

The MOVE Framework

Every programme follows four rigorous stages. No system advances without field-validated evidence.

M

MVP Build

Smallest deployable system addressing a real problem

O

Observe

Deploy with real users, collect field data

V

Validate

Independent review, failing programmes terminated

E

Expand

Scale into service, industry, and export
