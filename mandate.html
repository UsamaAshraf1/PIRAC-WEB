Mandate & Authority

PRAC operates as a delivery authority with clear decision rights, accountability, and termination powers, reporting at the highest level of government.

01
Defining concrete, high-impact national problems in AI, robotics, and autonomy
02
Authority over programme selection, funding release, technical validation, and termination
03
Appointing empowered Programme Managers responsible for delivery and coordination
04
Terminating non-performing efforts early and scaling only validated systems
05
Transitioning successful systems into defence service, industrial adoption, and export pathways
06
Building and maintaining a national AI and robotics talent pool aligned to priority sectors
07
Administering incentives strictly tied to deployment, validation, and scale of certified systems
